The Datacenter Manager

The Data Center Manager is final project delivered by Ewerton Vasconcelos da Silva for conclusion of the course of Electronics and Computing Engineering at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, oriented by the Professor Rodrigo de Souza Couto. The project meant to be a software and hardware solution to the management groups of servers in data centers that doesn't originally have the well-knows BMC (Baseboard Management Controllers) chips integrated on its motherboards. The Datacenter Manager Solves a set of issues and inconveniences on daily server management activities, providing a set of features like video remote console, keyboard, media mount and others.
